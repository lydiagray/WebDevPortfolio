About Me

My name is Lydia Gray and I love tea. If you've ever seen the film Scott Pilgrim vs The World, I'm a bit like Ramona Flowers (though I don't change my hair colour quite as frequently as she does). I always have at least 10 different types of tea in the house.

As well as brewing lots of tea, I like to try my hand at baking every so often. I love watching The Great British Bake Off and I have been known to try and replicate some of the challenges (with varying degrees of success).

I'm also a bit of an amateur photographer. I would describe my style as abstract landscape; I like to view things in an unconventional way and find interesting or unusual shapes and perspectives, experimenting with focus and depth-perception.

Oh, and I almost forgot, I'm also training to be a software engineer right now so I spend a lot of time studying about coding and design.

Find me on Facebook or Github to see what I'm up to.
